🏛️ About the Institution

A Dedicated
Health Sciences
University

"Training the health professionals who will define Nigeria's medical future."

The Federal University of Health Sciences, Ila-Orangun (FUHSI) is a specialized federal university established to address Nigeria's growing need for specialized health sciences education. Located in Ila-Orangun, Osun State, FUHSI offers focused academic programmes exclusively in health and medical sciences.

As one of Nigeria's dedicated health sciences universities, FUHSI is committed to producing graduates who are not only academically excellent but clinically and professionally competent to serve Nigeria's healthcare system at the highest levels.

The institution operates under the supervision of the Federal Ministry of Education and is accredited by the National Universities Commission (NUC), ensuring all programmes meet national and international academic standards.

2021

University Established

FUHSI founded by the Federal Government of Nigeria as a dedicated health sciences institution.

2021

First Academic Session

Inaugural academic session commenced with pioneer students across founding departments.

2026

NUC Full Accreditation

National Universities Commission granted full accreditation to FUHSI's academic programmes.

2024

Teaching Hospital Establishment Year

The Federal University of Health Sciences, Ila-Orangun (FUHSI), established its teaching hospital partnership through the creation of the Federal University of Health Sciences Teaching Hospital, Ila-Orangun, which began its development and setup process around 2024 and 2025

2025

Programme Expansion

As of May 2026, the university secured approval for additional courses expected to commence, including Doctor of Pharmacy, Public Health, , Anatomy, Physiology, and Radiography.
