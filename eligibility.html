How FUHSI Calculates Your Aggregate
The 3 documented formulas behind this report
Method 1 · Legacy 80:20
UTME/400 × 80  +  O'Level /20
O'Level scale: A1=4 · B2=3.5 · B3=3 · C4=2 · C5=2.5 · C6=1 · D7/E8/F9=0. Points from 5 subjects are summed directly (max 20). Observed roughly three admission cycles ago.
Method 2 · Current 80:20Most Recent
UTME/400 × 80  +  (O'Level sum/30) × 20
Updated O'Level scale: A1=6 · B2=5 · B3=4 · C4=3 · C5=2 · C6=1 · D7/E8/F9=0. Five subjects summed (max 30), then scaled to 20. FUHSI's most recently used formula.
Method 3 · 75:25 VariantUnconfirmed
UTME/400 × 75  +  (O'Level sum/20) × 25
Same O'Level scale as Method 1 (max 20) but scaled to 25 points (not 20). Shifts more weight onto O'Level performance. Observed approximately two cycles ago; never officially confirmed.

FUHSI has not published one permanent aggregate formula — the weighting and O'Level scale have changed across recent admission cycles. This report calculates your aggregate all three documented ways to show you the full realistic range rather than a single potentially wrong figure.
