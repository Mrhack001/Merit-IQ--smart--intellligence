❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using FUHSI MeritIQ

How is the FUHSI aggregate score calculated?
+

At Federal University of Health Sciences, Ila-Orangun, aggregate score is calculated by combining your UTME performance and O’Level grades using a weighted formula. Under the 80:20 system, stronger emphasis is placed on JAMB score. Under the 75:25 system, more weight is given to O’Level excellence.

What is the FUHSI screening process?
+

FUHSI uses a screening process that combines your JAMB score and O'Level grades. There is no separate Post-UTME exam. The screening helps the university assess qualified candidates based on merit and course requirements. Check FUHSI's official portal for screening dates and procedures.

Does MeritIQ guarantee my admission?
+

No. MeritIQ is an intelligence and prediction tool — it does not guarantee admission. Our predictions are based on historical data and current trends, but FUHSI's final admission decisions are made independently by the university. Use MeritIQ to make informed decisions, not as a guarantee.

How accurate are the admission predictions?
+

Our model has historically been accurate within a small margin (typically ±2-3 points) for most courses. Accuracy depends on data availability, course demand shifts, and special admissions categories. Medicine and Nursing predictions tend to be most accurate due to larger historical data sets.

What are alternative course recommendations?
+

If your aggregate score doesn't meet the merit threshold for your preferred course, MeritIQ suggests related FUHSI courses where your score has a higher likelihood of success. These are based on merit trends, subject compatibility, and career paths. You can explore different possibilities and make a strategic choice.

How often is FUHSI admission data updated?
+

We update our data as soon as FUHSI releases new admission information, including screening dates, merit scores, and policy changes. Historically, FUHSI releases merit information after each admission cycle. Users can subscribe to real-time notifications for instant updates on important announcements.

What is ELDS and how does it help my admission chances?
+

ELDS stands for Educationally Less Developed States. Students from ELDS states may receive admission advantages at some universities. MeritIQ's ELDS checker helps you verify if you qualify and how it might impact your admission probability at FUHSI. Check your home state and region for eligibility.

Can I use MeritIQ to compare multiple courses?
+

Yes! MeritIQ's Aspirant Dashboard allows you to save multiple predictions, track scenarios across different courses, and compare your admission probability for each program. This is especially useful if you're undecided between courses or want to explore safer alternatives based on your merit score.

What O'Level subjects should I have for FUHSI courses?
+

Admission into the Federal University of Health Sciences, Ila-Orangun (FUHSI) Federal University of Health Sciences, Ila-Orangun requires candidates to possess the appropriate O’Level subject combination relevant to their chosen course of study. In most cases, applicants must have five (5) credit passes, including English Language, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, and Physics, obtained in not more than two sittings. .

How can I contact FUHSI for admission inquiries?
+

For official admission inquiries, contact FUHSI directly through their official website (fuhsi.edu.ng), admission office email, or visit the campus in Ila-Orangun, Osun State, Nigeria. MeritIQ is an independent platform and cannot process official admission applications — always verify information with FUHSI's official channels.
